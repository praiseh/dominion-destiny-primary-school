Common Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

1 How do I apply for admission?
Apply online through our website or visit the school office to collect an application form. The admissions process runs throughout the year and our team is happy to guide you through each step.
2 What are the school fees?
Please contact our admissions office or email us for a detailed fee structure. Fees vary by grade level and flexible payment plans are available for qualifying families.
3 Can I schedule a school tour?
Yes! We warmly welcome prospective parents and students for guided tours. Contact us to book your visit during office hours and a member of staff will show you around.
4 What documents are needed for enrolment?
You will need a birth certificate, previous school reports, immunisation records, and two passport-sized photographs. Contact us for a full enrolment checklist tailored to your child's grade.
