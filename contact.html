Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How do I apply for admission?

You can apply online through our website or visit the school office to collect an application form. The admissions process runs throughout the year.

What are the school fees?

Please contact our admissions office or email us for a detailed fee structure. Fees vary by grade level and payment plans are available.

Can I schedule a school tour?

Yes! We welcome prospective parents and students for guided tours. Contact us to book a tour during office hours.

What documents are needed?

Birth certificate, previous school reports, immunization records, and passport-sized photos. Contact us for a complete checklist.
