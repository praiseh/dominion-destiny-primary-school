Our Foundation

Guided by Purpose & Values

Everything we do at Dominion Destiny flows from three core commitments that shape our school culture every day.

Our Mission

To provide an exceptional, child-centred education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, builds character, and prepares every learner for a purposeful life.

Our Vision

To be the leading primary school in Zimbabwe, recognised for transformative learning outcomes and a community built on excellence, love, and integrity.

Our Values

Respect, Responsibility, Resilience and Relationships. These four pillars guide how we teach, how we treat one another, and how we grow together.
