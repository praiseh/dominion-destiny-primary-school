Our Foundation

Mandated to Serve, Destined for Greatness

Our Mission

To provide quality education in excellence in the context of a Christian world view that makes a valuable contribution towards a stable and vibrant nation by equipping children today to be head and shoulders above their peers, with the skills, attitudes and values critical for their future survival and prosperity.

Our Vision

To be an icon of excellence in the provision of quality primary education in Zimbabwe and thus significantly contribute to its vision of achieving an informed and educated citizen.

Our Values

  • Integrity
